Computational Intelligence: Theory and Application

Department of Computer Science, University of Jaffna

12-14 July 2023

Introduction

The three days summer school aims to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of the principles and applications of computational intelligence. The target group for this program comprises individuals who are enthusiastic about exploring and expanding their knowledge in this field. Whether you have a basic understanding or a solid background in computer science, mathematics, or related disciplines, this summer school offers valuable insights and hands-on experience. At this summer school, we take pride in assembling a faculty of experienced academics renowned for their contributions to computational intelligence theories and applications, as well as industry personnel with hands-on experience in real-world applications. This unique combination of academic rigor and industry-driven training guarantees an exceptional learning opportunity for all participants. Participants specializing in computational intelligence, artificial intelligence, machine learning, or related fields can benefit from this summer school. The programme also provides an opportunity to explore the latest advancements, engage in interdisciplinary discussions, and collaborate with experts and fellow researchers.

Target Group

The target group for the summer school on computational intelligence includes undergraduate and postgraduate students, researchers, academics, industry professionals, as well as enthusiasts who want to expand their knowledge and skills in this rapidly evolving field. Regardless of your background or level of expertise, this programme offers a valuable learning experience and an opportunity to engage with experts and peers in the computational intelligence community
