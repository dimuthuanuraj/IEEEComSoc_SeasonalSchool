Prof. Gihan Dias

Prof. Dias graduated from the University of Moratuwa in 1985 and was awarded a doctorate in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from The University of California, Davis in 1992.

In addition to being a Professor in Computer Science and Engineering and Director, National Languages Processing Centre at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka he serves as the Domain Registrar of the LK Domain Registry and an Advisor to TechCERT. He was inducted to the Internet Hall of Fame in 2013.

He has been active in several areas of computing, including computer networking, language technologies, e-learning, security, Internet identity and cloud. He is currently developing resources, technologies, and tools to allow us to use IT in Sinhala and Tamil, including SiTa, the trilingual translation system among Sinhala, Tamil, and English.
