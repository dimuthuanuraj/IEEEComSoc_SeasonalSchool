Mr. Balasundram Arunn

Mr. Balasundram Arunn holds a MSc degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Moratuwa. Mr. Arunn is a highly skilled and accomplished Machine Learning Engineer, currently serving as the Lead Machine Learning Engineer at SenzMate. With a decade of academic and industrial experience, Mr. Arunn has honed his expertise in various domains, including sensor designing, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Predictive Modeling.

Mr. Arunn's key strength lies in his ability to solve complex problems by harnessing the power of Machine Learning and Deep Learning. He is adept at leveraging state-of-the-art techniques and algorithms to develop innovative solutions that drive tangible outcomes. His work involves the entire lifecycle of machine learning projects, from data preprocessing and feature engineering to model development, training, and deployment. With a relentless pursuit of staying at the forefront of technological advancements, Mr. Arunn remains actively engaged in the research community. They are passionate about continuously expanding their knowledge and exploring emerging trends in the field.
